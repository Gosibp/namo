NAMO — National Mission for Oncology

Bringing holistic cancer care within reach.

Our vision: holistic cancer care within reach of 15 crore citizens by 2030 — through awareness, early detection, screening and cure.

A pan-India effort, building cancer care community by community — from the heartland to every corner of the country.

See our plan
The Problem

Cancer in India is rising fast, found too late, and out of reach for most people.

Cancer now touches nearly every Indian family. But three problems stand in the way of beating it: the disease is growing faster than our ability to fight it, most cases are caught too late to cure, and the hospitals that can treat it are crowded into a few big cities — far from where most people live.

A growing burden
1.9 million

new cancer cases every year — and 1 in 9 Indians will face cancer in their lifetime.

Caught too late
7 in 10

cases are found at an advanced stage. Caught early, 9 in 10 patients survive — caught late, only 1 in 7 do.

Out of reach
95%

of cancer centres are in cities, while 7 in 10 Indians live in rural areas — where death rates are twice as high.

Our Solution · NAMO-CAN

A network of cancer centres that brings care closer to home

The National Mission of Oncology – Cancer Access Network (NAMO-CAN) is building a connected network of our own cancer centres across the country — so that no one has to travel far or wait long for care.

Our Plan

Four ways we reach people

A simple, connected path — from spreading awareness, to finding cancer early, to treating it close to home, to staying with families through recovery.

Awareness

Community ambassadors, youth volunteers and theatre-based campaigns that bring cancer awareness into everyday life, district by district.

Early Detection & Screening

Regular screening close to where people live, so cancers are found early — when treatment works best and costs least.

A Network of Cancer Centres

Connected hospitals and cancer centres so quality treatment is available closer to home, not only in big cities.

Community & Follow-up Care

Staying with patients and families through treatment and recovery, with local volunteers who keep care going.

Citizen and youth participation

Every ambassador and youth volunteer we train gains a skill and a role in their community — so the mission also builds local livelihoods as it grows.

Our vision
